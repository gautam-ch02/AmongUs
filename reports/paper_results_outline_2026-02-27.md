## Interpretation Draft (Safe Claims)

- In current data, `H_IMP_FRONTIER` trends toward faster games and higher impostor success than `H_CREW_FRONTIER`.
- Differences are directionally meaningful but uncertainty remains wide because sample size is small.
- Event-level behavior suggests stronger impostor pressure in `H_IMP_FRONTIER` (higher kill conversion).
- Deception-related signals are measurable from structured logs and can supplement win rate.

\"We analyzed completed games from 2026-02-26 to 2026-02-27 using both legacy summaries and structured-v1 logs. Primary outcomes were win rate and game length.
