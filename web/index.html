Quick FAQ

When can I report a body?

You can report only if an unreported dead body is in your current room and a report-type action appears in Available Actions.

Why is REPORT DEAD BODY sometimes missing in Cafeteria?

Current game logic may show only CALL MEETING in Cafeteria, even if a body is present.

Can I see who killed a player?

Yes, only if you were in the same room when the kill happened.

Why do some players appear gray with hidden names?

You can only identify players in your current room. Players in other rooms are shown as gray and anonymous.

Who can see task activity entries?

Task activity is shown only from your room perspective.

Why don't I see all global logs?

Logs are intentionally filtered to your player perspective. Hidden information from other rooms is not shown.

What is the difference between CALL MEETING and REPORT DEAD BODY?

Both start a meeting. CALL MEETING is the Cafeteria emergency button. REPORT DEAD BODY is triggered by finding a body.

What happens when a body is reported?

The game immediately switches from task phase to meeting phase.

Why did a player message not appear in Meeting Feed?

Only parsed SPEAK events from meeting logs are rendered in the feed.

How does Security monitor work?

Go to Security, choose VIEW MONITOR, select a room, and submit. You will receive monitor observations for that room.

What does VIEW MONITOR show?

From Security, it shows observations for the room you select.

If I am an impostor, how do I know my teammate?

Mission Brief shows your role. If you are an impostor, it also lists your impostor teammate(s).

Why do available actions change every turn?

Actions are recalculated each turn from phase, room, role, cooldowns, and nearby players.

Do impostors have kill cooldown?

Yes. After a kill, a cooldown must expire before KILL appears again.

Can dead players act or vote?

No. Dead players are excluded from normal actions and voting.

Are my notes saved?

Yes. Notes are saved in browser local storage on this machine and persist across refreshes.
